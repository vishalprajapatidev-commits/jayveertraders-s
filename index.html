Our Story

Jayveer Traders – From Humble Beginnings to a Trusted Name in Quality Tiles and Stones

Founded in 2020 by Shri Juharmalji Chatraji Prajapati, Jayveer Traders was built on the strong values of trust, honesty, and quality. What began as a vision driven by hard work and dedication has today grown into a reliable name in the tiles and natural stone market.

Currently managed by his sons Jagdishbhai Prajapati, Dilipbhai Prajapati, and Vikrambhai Prajapati, the business continues to grow with a strong focus on customer satisfaction, humble human values, and long-term relationships. The showroom offers a carefully selected range of premium tiles, granite, and marble, delivering the best quality at the most competitive prices compared to the market. With a customer-first approach and transparent dealings, Jayveer Traders has earned the trust and loyalty of clients across the region.
